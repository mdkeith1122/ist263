Bio

Maddie Keith is a second-year undergraduate student at Syracuse University, and is currently studying film production in the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications with a minor in Information Management and Technology in the iSchool. Her passion for film started at a very young age when she would hang out on the small sets of music videos and short films that her dad was always a part of. In high school, she followed the advanced film track, where she began to explore the ways to critically analyze film. At Syracuse, she has taken classes where she continued to apply this knowledge of analytical writing and learned even more about what techniques make film good. Maddie hopes to continue to learn about film and apply that knowledge when making her own films in the future.
